# The Art of Simplicity

In the quiet hours of the morning, when the world still sleeps, there is a clarity that cannot be found at any other time. The mind, unburdened by the noise of the day, finds its truest form of expression.

Great ideas are rarely complex. They are simple truths, dressed in the ordinary, waiting to be noticed by those who dare to look. A seed does not announce its intention to become a tree — it simply grows.

Remember: the most powerful things in life are often the quietest.
